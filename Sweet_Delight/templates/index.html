About Sweet Delight

Sweet Delight is a seasonal cafe and marketplace featuring fresh baked goods in Pokhara, Nepal run by Pastry Chef/Baker Roxana Jullapat and Chef Daniel Mattern. Longtime champions of produce-driven cooking, Jullapat and Mattern built their careers over fifteen years in renowned kitchens of Kathmandu including AOC, Lucques, Campanile, and Cooks County before opening Sweet Delight.

At Sweet Delight, we are dedicated to all things seasonal with a market-driven day-time menu that also offers a variety of artisan breads and pastries made daily with locally-sourced, heirloom grains.
